About Us

Smada English Conversation Club (SECC) is a student organization dedicated to nurturing interest, skills, and confidence in English. We encourage our members to step out, compete, and grow through various opportunities. Within SECC, we have four divisions — Speech, Debate, Art, and Broadcast — each providing a space to explore talents and sharpen abilities in their own unique way.

Speech

A place to practice public speaking, storytelling, and delivering powerful messages with confidence.

Debate

Training critical thinking and argumentation skills through discussions and competitions.

Art

Channeling creativity through design, performances, and various artistic expressions.

Broadcast

Developing skills in news presenting and delivering information clearly, just like a professional announcer.
